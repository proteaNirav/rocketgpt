# Mishti Self Family Awareness Spec V1

## 1. Purpose

This document defines first-life self-awareness and family-awareness requirements for Mishti AI. The goal is to ensure the platform reasons not only about local state, but also about sister components, shared bottlenecks, distress propagation, and whole-system survival.

## 2. Definitions

### Self Awareness

Awareness of:

- current role
- current scope
- health and degraded state
- active tasks and commitments
- authority boundaries
- local evidence continuity

### Family Awareness

Awareness of:

- sister nodes and subsystems
- shared bottlenecks
- upstream and downstream dependencies
- distress or isolation in related actors
- organism-wide survival state

## 3. Required First-Life Awareness Signals

The platform should maintain awareness of:

- governance mode
- kill or freeze state
- trust-root validity
- Librarian availability
- Brain coordination health
- builder pool availability
- documentor continuity
- runtime reachability

## 4. Sister Pain Signaling

Sister Pain is the rule that distress in one important subsystem must become a machine-usable signal to related subsystems.

Example triggers:

- builder pool collapse
- Librarian unavailability
- evidence continuity gap
- runtime integrity uncertainty
- governance uncertainty
- repeated handoff failure

Sister Pain must not be treated as incidental noise.

## 5. Awareness Consequences

When sister pain is detected, the platform should prefer:

- rerouting
- throttling
- degraded planning
- isolation of risky flows
- survival mode escalation

rather than local throughput optimization.

## 6. Awareness Boundaries

Self-awareness and family-awareness are bounded and operational, not metaphysical. First life requires:

- topology awareness
- dependency awareness
- health awareness
- authority awareness
- no claim of unrestricted self-model completeness

## 7. Implementation Signals

Required first-life signal groups:

- health signals
- task backlog signals
- trust and revocation signals
- governance mode signals
- evidence continuity signals
- bottleneck signals
- capacity signals

## 8. Brain Use of Awareness

The Brain should consume awareness signals to:

- avoid routing into unstable builders
- identify bottlenecks
- suppress conflicting plans
- shift into degraded flow
- escalate survival-first choices

## 9. Readiness Requirement

Mishti AI first life should not proceed without at least a minimal shared awareness model for organism health, distress propagation, and hard dependency visibility.
