# Mishti AI Self-Learning Snapshot Policy

## Purpose
This document defines the initial policy for publishing self-knowledge snapshots.

## Snapshot Principles
- snapshots are versioned
- snapshots are reviewable
- snapshots preserve evidence lineage
- snapshots represent accepted learning only
- snapshots do not imply direct runtime self-modification

## Constraint
Snapshots are governed artifacts for institutional memory, not autonomous change instructions.
