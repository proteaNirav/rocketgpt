# Mishti AI Builder Promotion Policy

## Purpose
This document defines the initial promotion posture for builder trust evolution.

## Promotion Path
External Builders may progress toward Internal status only through evidence-based review.

Required evidence areas:
- performance
- reliability
- security
- governance compliance
- background checks
- long-run stability

## Promotion Stages
1. validated external
2. trusted external
3. candidate internal
4. internal trusted

## Constraint
Promotion remains governed. No builder self-promotes and no promotion bypasses registration, wrapper mediation, reporting, or approval.
